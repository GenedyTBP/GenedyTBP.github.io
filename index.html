About Me

I'm Mohamed El-Genedy, a passionate product manager with over 5 years of experience turning innovative ideas into successful tech products. My journey began in software development, which gives me a unique technical perspective on product management.

I specialize in bridging the gap between business objectives and technical implementation, ensuring that every product I manage not only meets user needs but also drives significant business value. My approach combines data-driven decision making with creative problem-solving.

When I'm not building products, you'll find me sharing knowledge through my YouTube channel, writing case studies, and mentoring aspiring product managers.
